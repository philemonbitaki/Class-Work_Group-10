Leading Innovation in Technology Education

Welcome to Our Department

The Department of Computing and Informatics at Bugema University is a vibrant academic community dedicated to excellence in technology education and research. We prepare students to become innovative problem-solvers and leaders in the digital age.

Our department helps students build strong skills in software development, networking, web design, and information systems. Learners are introduced to modern tools that prepare them for class projects and real workplace challenges.

Students study both theory and practical work, which helps them understand how technology solves everyday problems. Group work, lab practice, and presentations also help students become confident communicators and team players.

Our goal is to prepare creative, disciplined, and job-ready graduates for the digital world.

Our Mission

To provide quality education in computing and informatics that prepares students for successful careers and lifelong learning in a rapidly changing technological world.

Our Vision

To be a leading department in computing and informatics education, recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and community service.

Our Values

Excellence, Innovation, Integrity, Collaboration, and Service to humanity through technology education.

  • Programming Lab (50 workstations)
  • Networking Lab (30 workstations)
  • Database Lab (40 workstations)
  • Multimedia Lab (35 workstations)

Department Library

Specialized collection of computing books, journals, and digital resources with comfortable study spaces.

  • 5,000+ books on computing topics
  • Digital access to research databases

  • 24/7 access for students

Creative space for students to work on projects, collaborate with peers, and develop innovative solutions.

  • 3D printing facilities
  • IoT development kits
  • VR/AR equipment
  • Project mentorship program
