Bachelor's Degree Programs

Bachelor of Computer Science

4 Years 240 Credits Full-time
Apply Now View Curriculum

The Bachelor of Computer Science program provides a comprehensive foundation in computer science theory and practice. Students learn programming, algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and emerging technologies like artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Key Features:

  • Strong foundation in mathematics and theoretical computer science
  • Extensive programming experience in multiple languages
  • Hands-on projects and industry internships
  • Research opportunities with faculty members
  • Preparation for graduate studies or industry careers

Career Opportunities:

Software Engineer Data Scientist AI Specialist Systems Architect Research Scientist DevOps Engineer

Admission Requirements:

  • UACE with 2 principal passes in Mathematics and Physics/Computer Science
  • UCE with at least 5 credits including Mathematics and English
  • Minimum aggregate of 6 points at UACE
  • Pass departmental interview and aptitude test

Tuition Fees:

Ugandan Students: UGX 2,500,000 per semester

International Students: USD 1,200 per semester

Additional Costs: Lab fees, textbooks, and project materials

Curriculum Overview:

Year 1
  • Introduction to Programming
  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Computer Fundamentals
  • Digital Logic
  • Communication Skills
  • Calculus I
Year 2
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Object-Oriented Programming
  • Computer Architecture
  • Database Systems
  • Web Development
  • Linear Algebra
Year 3
  • Software Engineering
  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Cybersecurity
  • Research Methods
Year 4
  • Machine Learning
  • Cloud Computing
  • Mobile App Development
  • Project Management
  • Final Year Project
  • Industrial Training

Bachelor of Information Technology

4 Years 240 Credits Full-time
Apply Now View Curriculum

The Bachelor of Information Technology program focuses on the practical application of technology in business and organizational settings. Students learn to design, implement, and manage IT solutions that meet organizational needs.

Key Features:

  • Focus on practical IT skills and business applications
  • Network administration and system management
  • Database design and administration
  • IT project management and consulting
  • Business analysis and requirements gathering

Career Opportunities:

IT Manager Network Administrator Database Administrator Systems Analyst IT Consultant Business Analyst

Admission Requirements:

  • UACE with 2 principal passes in any subjects
  • UCE with at least 5 credits including Mathematics and English
  • Minimum aggregate of 8 points at UACE
  • Pass departmental interview

Tuition Fees:

Ugandan Students: UGX 2,200,000 per semester

International Students: USD 1,000 per semester

Additional Costs: Lab fees, textbooks, and certification exams

Diploma Programs

Diploma in Web Development

2 Years 120 Credits Full-time
Apply Now View Curriculum

The Diploma in Web Development program provides comprehensive training in modern web technologies. Students learn to create responsive, interactive websites and web applications using current industry standards and best practices.

Key Features:

  • Hands-on training in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Modern frameworks like React and Angular
  • Backend development with Node.js and PHP
  • Database integration and API development
  • Portfolio development and real projects

Career Opportunities:

Frontend Developer Backend Developer Full-Stack Developer Web Designer UI/UX Developer Freelance Developer

Admission Requirements:

  • UCE with at least 5 credits including Mathematics and English
  • Basic computer literacy
  • Pass departmental interview
  • Age 16 and above

Tuition Fees:

Ugandan Students: UGX 1,500,000 per semester

International Students: USD 700 per semester

Additional Costs: Web hosting, domain names, and project materials
