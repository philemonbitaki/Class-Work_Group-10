Getting to Bugema University

By Public Transport

  • From Kampala: Take taxi to Bugema town (approximately 2 hours)
  • From Entebbe: Take taxi to Kampala, then connect to Bugema
  • From Jinja: Direct taxis available to Bugema
  • Campus is 5 minutes walk from Bugema town center

By Private Vehicle

  • From Kampala: Take Jinja Road, turn at Lugazi towards Bugema
  • Distance: Approximately 60km from Kampala
  • Travel time: 1.5 - 2 hours depending on traffic
  • Ample parking available on campus

From Entebbe Airport

  • Take airport taxi to Kampala (approximately 1 hour)
  • Connect to Bugema taxi from Kampala taxi park
  • Total travel time: 3 - 4 hours
  • Airport pickup service available for international students

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to the Department of Computing and Informatics?

You can apply by visiting our registration page and filling out the application form. Alternatively, you can visit our admissions office in person or download the application form from our website.

What are the admission requirements for the programs?

Requirements vary by program. For bachelor's degrees, you need UACE with 2 principal passes and UCE with at least 5 credits. For diplomas, UCE with at least 4-5 credits is required. Please check our programs page for specific requirements.

When is the application deadline?

The application deadline for the August intake is May 31st, and for the January intake is October 31st. Late applications may be considered subject to availability.

Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?

Yes, we offer various scholarships including academic excellence scholarships, need-based financial aid, sports scholarships, and women in technology scholarships. Please contact our admissions office for more information.

What is the student-to-faculty ratio?

We maintain a student-to-faculty ratio of approximately 20:1, ensuring personalized attention and quality education for our students.

Are there internship opportunities for students?

Yes, we have partnerships with over 15 tech companies that provide internship opportunities for our students. We also have a dedicated career development office that helps students secure internships and job placements.
